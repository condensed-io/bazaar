An Event Series for Local Makers

A Holiday Affair Like No Other

Brooklyn Holiday Bazaar is a unique annual event that showcases the best of NYC-based makers. Our events are packed with fine handmade goods, delicious food & drinks, music, craft activities and good times for the whole family.

Celebrating Local Businesses

Brooklyn Holiday Bazaar is presented by Cut & Crafted, formerly Brooklyn Makers. We launched in 2012 to tell the stories behind remarkable local brands & entrepreneurs. Our annual holiday event is a celebration of the creative talent that Brooklyn has to offer.
